What's New in v4.7.5?

🤖

Autopilot Mode

When queue ends, bot automatically adds 2-5 similar tracks after 30s delay. Analyzes last 3 tracks from history to match genre and style. Never stop the music!

🏆

30 Achievements

Reward system for activity: listen to music, collect unique genres, use random and get achievements from "First Steps" to "Couch Expert" (500h listening time).

📊

Personal Stats

Command /stats shows: tracks played, total listening time, top-5 favorite genres, achievement progress and how much left to next reward.

🌐

Global Top

See what's playing on all servers. Global track chart (/globaltop) and server user leaderboard (/top) by tracks added and listening time.

👁️

7 Rarity Levels

micro 1K-50K (Underground)
small 50K-250K (Growing)
medium 250K-1M (Medium)
large 1M-100M (Popular)
mega 100M+ (Viral)
deep 1K-500K (Deep classic)
!random micro or /random rarity:micro

⚡

Hybrid Commands

All commands work as both !play and /play. Choose your preferred format. Slash commands with autocomplete parameters.

🌍

Multilingual

Bot auto-detects server language (RU/EN). Manual switch: /language or !language. All commands, responses and achievements fully translated.

🎲

Smart Random v4.7

Combines base tags with year, style and mood modifiers. Anti-random filter blocks karaoke and instrumentals. Doesn't repeat last 20 genres.

Commands v4.7.5 HYBRID

Terminal - v4.7.5 Autopilot Update Commands
!start
/start
Connects bot to voice channel and shows control panel with buttons
!play [query or link]
/play query:[query]
Searches YouTube and adds to queue. Example: !play phonk 2024 or /play query:phonk 2024
!stats
/stats
Shows personal statistics:
• Tracks played: 1337
• Listening time: 42h 15min
• Top genres: phonk (25%), rock (20%), electronic (15%)
• Achievements unlocked: 12/30
• Next achievement: Music Lover (8h left)
!top
/top
Server user leaderboard:
🥇 @user1 — 245 tracks (68h)
🥈 @user2 — 189 tracks (45h)
🥉 @user3 — 156 tracks (38h)
Your position: #5 (89 tracks, 22h)
!globaltop
/globaltop
Global chart of popular tracks:
1. Artist - Track Name (152 plays)
2. Another - Song (128 plays)
3. Phonk - Brazil (98 plays)
...across all servers with the bot
!autopilot [on/off/status]
/autopilot mode:[on/off/status] ADMIN
Toggle autopilot mode (admin only):
• on — Bot auto-adds tracks when queue ends (30s delay)
• off — Manual queue management
• status — Check current setting
Analyzes last 3 tracks to match genre/style
!language [ru/en]
/language lang:[ru/en]
Change bot language. Affects all responses, achievements and commands.
!random [rarity]
/random rarity:[micro/small/medium/large/mega/deep/any]
Adds random track by view filter:
• micro 1K-50K (Underground)
• small 50K-250K (Growing)
• medium 250K-1M
• large 1M-100M (Popular)
• mega 100M+ (Viral)
• deep 1K-500K (classic)
• any — any views (default)
!addrandom [amount] [rarity]
/addrandom count:[N] rarity:[rarity]
Adds multiple random tracks. Example: !addrandom 5 micro or /addrandom count:5 rarity:micro
!queue
/queue
Shows current queue with live progress bar for playing track, history and control buttons (multi-remove up to 10 tracks)
!search [query]
/search query:[query]
Searches YouTube for 5 options, lets you choose by number (1-5)
!skip / !previous
/skip | /previous
Skips current track or returns previous from history (20 last tracks)
!loop [off/one/all]
/loop mode:[off/one/all]
Loop mode:
• one — repeat current track
• all — repeat entire queue
• off — disabled (default)
!shuffle
/shuffle
Shuffles queue in random order
!remove [number]
/remove position:[number]
Removes track from queue by position. For multi-remove use 🗑️ button in queue panel (up to 10 tracks)
!fund
/fund
Shows Raspberry Pi fund status: progress bar, raised amount, top donors. Donate 100₽ = your tag in random!
!pause / !resume / !stop
/pause | /resume | /stop
Basic controls: pause, resume, full stop and disconnect
